Earth - Our Blue Planet

Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only astronomical object known to harbor life. About 71% of Earth's surface is covered with water, mostly by its oceans, with the remainder consisting of continents and islands.

Interesting Facts About Earth

  • Diameter: about 12,742 km (7,918 miles)
  • Age: Approximately 4.54 billion years old
  • Atmosphere: Mainly nitrogen (78%), oxygen (21%), and trace gases
  • Has one natural satellite - the Moon
  • Surface: Oceans, continents, mountains, forests, deserts, and polar ice caps
  • Supports diverse ecosystems and life forms
